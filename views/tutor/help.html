FAQ


Question: How do I log my hours and get paid?


Answer: Right now, it’s a three step process. First, when you go to a session, take a student-tutor invoice. Fill it out at the end of your session, and make sure your student signs it. Then, log the hours here in your online tutor portal under the “Tutoring” tab by clicking “Log Session.”
Lastly, send your invoice to Robb at badasstutors2015@gmail.com





Question: Will my address, phone number, and other private information be given out online?


Answer: Your residential address, last name, email, phone number, and hourly pay are all kept private! If you decide to accept a student, that student will be given your phone number and email address so you can contact each other to schedule a tutoring appointment.





Question: How can students search me by my location without giving out my address?


Answer: When you fill out the “max travel distance” section of your profile, that distance is used to decide whether or not you will show up in a search for in person tutors by location. Here’s how it works: Let’s say your max travel distance is 15 miles. When our customers type in their address, your profile will show up if your residential address is within 15 miles (as the bird flies) of the location they type in.
